# CENTER FOR ENERGY TRANSITION (CENTRA) - UAI - CHILE
Welcome to the organizational page of the Center for Energy Transition (CENTRA). CENTRA is a multidisciplinary center belonging to the Faculty of Engineering and Science in Universidad Adolfo Ibañez (UAI) in Chile.

## Mission
The UAI Center for Energy Transition (CENTRA UAI) promotes a cost-efficient, safe, socio-environmentally sustainable, and territorially just energy transition, through fundamental and applied, collaborative and interdisciplinary research, developing innovative tools for the public sector and industry.

## Vision
To be an applied research center based on cutting-edge research and technological innovation, providing strategic value and solutions that enable industry and the public sector to lead the energy transition.

# Activities
## Research
Our team conducts frontier research in energy and sustainable development, recognized by the industry and featured in leading scientific journals.

## Transfer
We carry out technical assistance, technology development, and transfer projects for public and private institutions, adding value to the national energy industry.

## Education
We train professional human capital to lead the energy transition process in both public and private organizations.
